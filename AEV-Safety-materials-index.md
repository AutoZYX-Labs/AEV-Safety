Extracted background:

- Autonomous construction machinery is framed as a response to smart mining, smart construction sites and smart ports.
- Loaders are work-oriented machines. Their core task is not only driving but digging, loading, unloading, measuring and repeating work cycles.
- Key technical difficulties include autonomous digging point selection, autonomous unloading point identification, bucket fill-rate measurement, path planning and tracking, material-pile perception and articulated-machine control.
