AEV

Autonomous Engineering Vehicle Safety

面向自主工程车辆、移动作业机器系统和未来星球资源开采的开放安全研究框架。

An open safety research framework for autonomous engineering vehicles, mobile working-machine systems and future planetary resource operations.

为什么需要 AEV Safety

Why AEV Safety

工程车辆不是只会移动的道路车辆。它们要挖、装、卸、破碎、打桩、运输、重构场地，还要承担工期、质量、资产和恢复能力风险。

Engineering vehicles are not road vehicles that only drive. They dig, load, unload, crush, pile, haul and reshape worksites, creating safety, quality, asset and recovery risks.

从 ODD 到 WTDC

From ODD to WTDC

用作业场地与任务设计条件描述地形、物料、工具、监督和数字基础设施。

Use worksite and task design conditions to describe terrain, material, tool, supervision and digital infrastructure boundaries.

从 DDT 到 DMWT

From DDT to DMWT

把动态驾驶任务扩展为移动和工作装置共同构成的动态作业任务。

Extend dynamic driving task into dynamic moving and working task.
